About Grahbook

The first AI-powered store bot built for Bharat. Commerce at the speed of WhatsApp.

Our Story

Grahbook was born from a simple observation: millions of Indian small business owners already use WhatsApp as their primary tool for customer communication. Yet, managing orders, payments, inventory, and billing through WhatsApp remained a painful, manual process.

We asked ourselves — what if WhatsApp itself became the store? What if a shopkeeper in Chandni Chowk or a boutique owner in Jaipur could run their entire business through the same app their customers already use?

Grahbook is that answer. We built an AI-powered store bot that transforms your WhatsApp into a fully functional commerce platform — from catalog browsing and order placement to UPI payments and GST invoicing. All without requiring your customers to download a single app.

Our Mission

To empower every Indian small business owner with the tools they need to compete in the digital economy — using the platform they already know and love: WhatsApp. We believe commerce should be as simple as sending a message.

What We Offer

Grahbook is a comprehensive WhatsApp-based commerce platform designed specifically for Indian merchants. Our product includes:

  • WhatsApp Store Bot: An AI-powered bot that handles customer queries, shows product catalogs, takes orders, and processes payments — all within WhatsApp chat.
  • Smart Billing & Invoicing: Automatic GST-compliant invoice generation, Tally export, and financial reporting. Prices are displayed and processed in Indian Rupees (INR).
  • UPI Payment Integration: Direct integration with GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps. Instant settlement to your bank account with no middleman.
  • Inventory Management: AI-powered low-stock alerts, regional trend analysis, and real-time inventory tracking.
  • Customer Relationship Management: Automatic customer profiling, purchase history tracking, and personalized follow-ups.
  • Multi-Language Support: Supports Hindi, English, and Hinglish — because your customers should be able to shop in the language they're comfortable with.
  • Merchant Dashboard: Real-time metrics, order management, and business insights through a web-based dashboard accessible at grahbook.in.
